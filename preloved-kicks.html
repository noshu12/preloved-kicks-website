OUR
STORY

We're two guys from Karachi who got tired of paying retail for sneakers that half the city already owns. So we started hunting — Sunday bazaars, thrift markets, word-of-mouth finds.

Every pair in our shop has been personally sourced, cleaned, and authenticated by us. No fakes. No surprises. Just real kicks at real prices.

Preloved Kicks isn't just a shop — it's a movement. We believe the best sneakers are the ones with a story. Cop yours today.
